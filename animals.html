Animals

Meet and Greet Them at the Manila Zoo!




Mammals

Elephant

An elephant is a large, intelligent mammal that is the largest land animal on Earth. Elephants are found in Africa and Asia and are known for their long trunks, large ears, and gentle nature.

Tiger

A tiger is the largest wild cat in the world. It is an apex predator that is found in Asia. Tigers are known for their orange fur with black stripes, their powerful build, and their strength.

Monkey

A monkey is a small to medium-sized primate that is found in Africa, Asia, and South America. Monkeys are known for their intelligence, their ability to climb trees, and their social behavior.

Deer

A deer is a hoofed ruminant mammal that is found in forests and woodlands all over the world. Deer are known for their graceful appearance, their antlers, and their gentle nature.

Avians

Eagle

An eagle is a large, powerful bird of prey with a sharp beak and talons. Eagles are found all over the world and are known for their hunting skills and majestic appearance.

Peafowl

Peafowl - A peafowl is a large, colorful bird that is known for its iridescent tail feathers. The male peafowl, or peacock, is more colorful than the female, or peahen.

Macaw

Macaws are a group of New World parrots that are long-tailed and often colorful. They are popular in aviculture or as companion parrots, although there are conservation concerns about several species in the wild

Dove

A dove is a small, plump bird that is related to the pigeon. Doves are often seen as symbols of peace and love.

Reptiles

Python

Python is a genus of constricting snakes in the Pythonidae family native to the tropics and subtropics of the Eastern Hemisphere. The name python was proposed by Fran√ßois Marie Daudin in 1803 for non-venomous flecked snakes. Currently, 10 python species are recognized as valid taxa.

Iguana

An iguana is a large, herbivorous lizard that is native to tropical areas of Mexico, Central America, South America, and the Caribbean. They are known for their long tails, dewlaps, and sharp teeth.

Tortoise

Tortoises are reptiles of the family Testudinidae of the order Testudines. Like other turtles, tortoises have a shell to protect from predation and other threats.

Lizard

A lizard is a scaly, cold-blooded reptile that has a long tail and four legs. Lizards are found all over the world and come in a variety of shapes and sizes.

Aquatic

Red Pacu

Piaractus brachypomus, the pirapitinga, is a large species of pacu, a close relative of piranhas and silver dollars, in the serrasalmid family.

Catfish

The giant pangasius, paroon shark, pangasid-catfish or Chao Phraya giant catfish is a species of freshwater fish in the shark catfish family of order Siluriformes, found in the Chao Phraya and Mekong basins in Indochina.

Koi

Koi, or more specifically nishikigoi, are colored varieties of the Amur carp that are kept for decorative purposes in outdoor koi ponds or water gardens. Koi is an informal name for the colored variants of C. rubrofuscus kept for ornamental purposes.

Gar

The alligator gar is an euryhaline ray-finned fish in the clade Ginglymodi of the infraclass Holostei, being most closely related to the bowfins. It is the largest species in the gar family, and is among the largest freshwater fish in North America.
